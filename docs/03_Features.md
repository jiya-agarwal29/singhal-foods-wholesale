## 4. Unique Features (USP)

### ⭐ Quick Reorder

Business customers can recreate previously placed orders with a single click, making recurring wholesale purchases faster and more convenient.

---

### ⭐ Business Notes

Customers can include delivery instructions or business-specific requests while placing an order. These notes are visible to administrators during order processing.

Examples:

- Deliver before 9:00 AM
- Send GST Invoice
- Separate fragile items

---

### ⭐ Smart Restock Suggestions

When inventory falls below a predefined threshold, the system recommends a suitable quantity to restock, helping administrators maintain adequate stock levels.

---

### ⭐ Low Stock Alerts

Products nearing stock depletion are automatically highlighted on the administrator dashboard.
